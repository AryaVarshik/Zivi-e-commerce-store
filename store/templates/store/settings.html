Frequently Asked Questions

How long does standard delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. For orders above $50.00, standard shipping is completely free!

What is Zivi's return policy?

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy on all unworn clothing and boxed products in their original packaging.

Can I send orders as a gift?

Yes! During checkout, simply toggle the "This order is a gift" checkbox to enter custom recipient details and a sweet card message. The invoice will exclude price figures.
